Longzhi Yuan

Currently, I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at Prof. Yuguang Fang's group in City University of Hong Kong. Before that, I received the Ph.D. degree under the supervision of Prof. Wei Gong at School of Data Science in University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2023 and the B.E. degree in Electronic & Information Engineering of USTC in 2017, respectively. Also I am appreciated to spend great two years with Prof. Weidong Chen during 2017-2019.

My research focuses on wireless networks and Internet-of-Things. I worked to develop low-power backscatter communication systems, improve their throughputs, and make them compatible with commercial radios.
